MemoryDialogueBot

Consent form and information

Description

This research is part of a Master Thesis Project in the topic of memory-based conversational agents. The agent is developed to be a question answering component, answering self-disclosure type questions. This is part of a research study on how people interact with a ‘personal question answering’ chatbot. After the conversation you will be asked to answer a number of questions. The questions are about rating how you perceive the agent in terms of its natural-ness (human-like), engagingness, etc., on a 5-point scale. You will also be asked to fill in your age and your estimated English proficiency.

Your participation will take approximately 4-7 minutes.

Risks and benefits

All messages sent to the chatbot will be saved and may be used by the current or a future version of the system. The stored messages may be used to extend the existing question/answer retrieval database. Data given in the survey may be presented in a Master Thesis report & presentation. There is no discomfort involved in this study, nor any risks. We cannot and do not guarantee or promise that you will receive any benefits from this study.

Requirements to participate

You must be 18 years or older to participate.
Basic conversational proficiency in the English language is desired.

You have the right to refuse to answer particular questions. You have the right to refuse afterwards (within 24 hours) to allow your data to be used for the research.
